LucidSeal — Brand Guide

Logo

Use the primary logotype and shield mark. Prefer SVG. Keep clear space and strong contrast.

Do: keep aspect ratio; ensure contrast; give breathing room.

Don’t: stretch, recolor, add effects, or place over busy imagery.

Typography

Base: 16px/1.6 from app.css. Scale tokens: --fs-h1, --fs-h2, --fs-h3.

  • Use short, plain sentences. Avoid jargon.
  • Links are underlined by default (distinct from brand colour).
  • Rely on the system-UI stack set in app.css.

Colour

Use your app tokens directly (no overrides).

Primary (accent)

Token: --accent · Secondary: --accent-2

Surfaces

Tokens: --panel, --panel-2 · Border: --ring

Text: --ink · Muted: --muted · Success: --success

  • Variants: dark (light bg), light (dark bg)

  • Do: use provided files; keep proportions
  • Don’t: recolor, distort, rotate, add effects
